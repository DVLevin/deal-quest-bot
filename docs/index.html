FAQ

Quick answers

Do I need to install anything? ▼
No. It's a Telegram bot. If you have Telegram (phone or desktop), you're good. Just search for @l2gd_bot and tap Start.
Does it cost money? ▼
No. The Quick Setup uses a shared team AI key that's free for you. Power users can optionally bring their own Claude API key for premium quality.
Can I use voice instead of typing? ▼
Yes! You can send voice messages for both /support and training scenarios. The bot transcribes your voice and evaluates your response. It's actually encouraged — real sales happen on calls, not keyboards.
How does scoring work? ▼
Each scenario has a rubric with weighted criteria (positioning, objection handling, brevity, etc.). The AI evaluates your response against these criteria and gives you a score out of 100, plus specific feedback on strengths and areas to improve.
Is my data private? ▼
Your API keys are encrypted with Fernet symmetric encryption before storage. Your conversations and prospect data are only visible to you (and team admins for aggregate analytics). Nothing is shared externally.
What if I get stuck? ▼
Type /cancel at any point to exit the current flow. Type /start to see the main menu. If something feels broken, reach out to your team lead or check the bot's /settings.
