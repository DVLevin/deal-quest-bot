## Buyer Types

### Corporate / Strategic Buyers
- **Profile:** Large companies looking for acquisitions
- **Key concerns:** Integration, team retention, IP quality
- **Approach:** Lead with strategic value, not price

### Financial Buyers (PE/VC)
- **Profile:** Investment firms seeking portfolio additions
- **Key concerns:** Returns, scalability, market position
- **Approach:** Lead with metrics and growth potential

### Founders / Operators
- **Profile:** Startup founders exploring options
- **Key concerns:** Team culture, mission alignment, legacy
- **Approach:** Lead with vision alignment

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response Framework |
|-----------|-------------------|
| "Too expensive" | Reframe around value / ROI |
| "We have our own team" | Position as complement |
| "Never heard of you" | Social proof + specific results |
| "Bad timing" | Create urgency with market trends |
